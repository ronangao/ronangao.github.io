Summary

The American Society of Plastic Surgeons has just released the annual plastic surgery statistics report of the year 2013 on Feb 26, which shows a steady growth of plastic surgery procedures in U.S. However, the numbers of certain types of procedures have significantly declined. Besides, the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery surveys plastic surgeons throughout the world to provide statistics about the number and type of aesthetic (cosmetic) procedures they performed over a one-year period. Together, the results revealed some interesting trends in aesthetic preferences as well as various health issues. The quest for beauty is universal. Yet the tastes can vary.

The project would be a (or several) chart(s) showing the countries with top number of procedures and procedures per capita. It would be interactive, allowing viewers to see the countries and types of surgeries they care about. Also I would like to bring the heat map onto a human body to illustrate which part of the body would most people want to change.

There has been few coverage about cross-country comparisons of plastic surgery, or coverage presented in the form of a heat map on a human body.
